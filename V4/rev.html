My Top Three

I have always enjoyed gaming, and I have played hundreds throughout the years. As you might be able to guess, I have found games that I do love. Here are my top three games:

    • The Legendof Zelda: Ocarina of time

    • Platforms:
      Nintendo 64, Game Cube, Nintendo 3DS
    • Year:
      1998
    • Play as the protagonist Link on his Quest to save the land of Hyrule from the Demon King Ganondorf.

    • Super Smash Bros: Ultimate

    • Platforms:
      Nintendo Switch
    • Year:
      2018
    • Play as over 70+ characters from the Nintendo games and other third party games in this all-star lineup.

    • The Last of US

    • Platforms:
      Play Station 3, Play Station 4
    • Year:
      2013
    • Play as Joel as he escorts a young girl named Ellie through a Post-apocalyptic world filled with fungus infected individuals.
